"SNEAKERS" is a burgeoning startup established by three bright engineering students: Soumya, Tushar, and Jasmine. All three are currently pursuing their Bachelor's degrees in Computer Science and Engineering at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankulathur. These individuals have distinguished themselves as top achievers and intellectual powerhouses in their field. Driven by their collective vision and entrepreneurial spirit, they embarked on a journey to create the "SNEAKERS" brand. Notably, the entire website for their venture was meticulously designed by this talented trio. Their startup has rapidly gained traction and emerged as a resounding success, particularly among the younger, sneaker-loving generation of today. "SNEAKERS" is now a recognized and celebrated name in the world of footwear and a testament to the creative prowess and determination of these aspiring entrepreneurs.
